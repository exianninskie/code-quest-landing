# Monetization Compliance Checklist

Hey Ninskie! Here’s a quick reference guide for your monetization strategy to keep everything running smoothly and legally.

## PayPal Setup
*   **Account Type:** Ensure your PayPal account is set up for **"Donations"** or **"Digital Goods"**. This helps category-correct your income and reduces the risk of merchant disputes or account freezes.
*   **Confirmation:** Once set up, verify your account to remove any withdrawal limits.

## Tax & VAT
*   **Disclosure:** Depending on where you live, "BESTOW" donations might be considered taxable income.
*   **Consulting:** If Code Quest starts bringing in significant "BESTOW" contributions, it’s a good idea to check with a local tax professional to see if you need to report it.

## Bestow Transparency
*   Keep the non-refundable nature of donations clear in the app UI (which I've already included in the Terms of Service).
*   Thank your donors! A simple message or special badge in the "Soul Link" goes a long way.

*Good luck with the BESTOW feature!*
