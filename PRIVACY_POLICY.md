# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 23, 2026

Welcome to **Code Quest**. Your privacy is a priority for us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data to ensure a secure and transparent experience.

## 1. Data Collection
We collect specific data to provide essential game features:
*   **Emails:** Used for account authentication and security.
*   **Usernames:** Used for your public identity within the world of Code Quest.
*   **Avatar Images:** Used to personalize your profile and interactions.

## 2. Activity Tracking (Soul Link)
To facilitate the "Soul Link" online status feature, the app records a "Last Active" heartbeat. This tracks when you were last active in the app so other players can see your online status.

## 3. Third-Party Sharing
We respect your personal space:
*   We **do not sell** your data to anyone.
*   All data is processed securely via **Supabase**, our trusted infrastructure provider.

## 4. User Rights
You are in control of your journey:
*   You may request to delete your account and all associated data at any time.
*   Account and data deletion is handled via Supabase Auth services.

## 5. Contact
For any privacy-related inquiries, reach out to Ninskie.
